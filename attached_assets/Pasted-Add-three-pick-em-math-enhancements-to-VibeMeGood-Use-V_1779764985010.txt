Formula for pickemEV:
EV = (leg1_prob × leg2_prob × ... × legN_prob) 
     × multiplier − 1

This is NOT sportsbook EV. Pick'em has fixed 
payouts so only the joint probability matters.

ENHANCEMENT 3 — Payout Shift Detection

Add detectPayoutShift() to pickem-math.ts:

function detectPayoutShift(legs: EntryLeg[]): {
  hasShift: boolean
  estimatedMultiplier: number
  warning: string | null
}

Rules for detecting payout shifts:

STRONG correlation — likely shift:
- Two players same team, same game, both overs
  for correlated stats (QB pass yards + WR 
  receiving yards)
- Two receivers same team both on receiving 
  yards over
- Two players same game high team total

NEGATIVE correlation — platform may adjust:
- QB rushing yards + WR receiving yards

When shift detected show warning:
"⚠️ Payout shift likely — PrizePicks may 
reduce your multiplier from 6x to 5.5-5.7x 
due to correlated legs. Actual EV may be 
lower than shown."

Show both:
- Standard EV (at full multiplier)
- Adjusted EV (at estimated reduced multiplier)

Show tip:
"To avoid payout shifts: mix teams, mix games, 
or take opposite sides on correlated players."

VERIFY all three with these tests:

Test 1: 3-pick entry, all legs at 55%
Expected: EV = 0%, break-even exactly met
Indicator: amber

Test 2: 2-pick entry, both legs at 60%
Expected: EV = 8%, above break-even of 57.7%
Indicator: green

Test 3: 2 receivers same team, both overs
Expected: payout shift warning shown
Adjusted EV lower than standard EV

Report pass or fail for all 3 tests.
