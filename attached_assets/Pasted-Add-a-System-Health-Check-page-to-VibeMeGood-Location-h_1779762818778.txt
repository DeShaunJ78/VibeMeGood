Add a System Health Check page to VibeMeGood.

Location: /health
Sidebar label: "⚡ System Status"
Pin it at the very top of the sidebar above 
all other navigation items.

This page runs a full diagnostic and shows
green/amber/red status for every component.
User clicks "Run Health Check" and sees 
results in under 10 seconds.

CHECKS TO RUN (all in parallel):

DATA FRESHNESS
- PP Lines: when was last PrizePicks sync?
  Green: under 2 hours
  Amber: 2-6 hours
  Red: over 6 hours or zero lines

- External Odds: when was last odds sync?
  Green: under 30 minutes
  Amber: 30 min - 2 hours
  Red: over 2 hours

- Projections: when last computed?
  Green: under 8 hours
  Amber: 8-16 hours
  Red: over 16 hours or never run

- Injury News: when last synced?
  Green: under 4 hours
  Amber: 4-12 hours
  Red: over 12 hours

- Variance Scores: when last computed?
  Green: computed today
  Amber: computed yesterday
  Red: over 48 hours or never run

DATABASE HEALTH
- Total PP lines:
  Green: above 1,000
  Amber: 100-1,000
  Red: under 100

- Players with projections:
  Green: above 100
  Amber: 10-100
  Red: zero (projections never run)

- External lines with trueEdge:
  Green: above 30
  Amber: 1-30
  Red: zero (no book data)

- Journal entries:
  Green: has entries (tracking started)
  Amber: empty (not started yet)

- Calibration records:
  Green: has records
  Amber: empty (not started yet)
