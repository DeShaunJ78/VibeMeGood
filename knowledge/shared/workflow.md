# Daily Pick'em Workflow

## Morning (6:30–9:00 AM)
1. Check sync logs — confirm lines, injuries, projections all updated
2. Review Variance Intelligence cron run (6:30 AM daily)
3. Scan Slate Board for Goblin lines with P(Over) > 60%
4. Check Injuries & News — filter any GTD/OUT players from consideration
5. Note any back-to-back players (high fatigue load)

## Pre-Slate (Lock −2h)
1. Confirm lineup news — avoid unconfirmed starters
2. Check blowout risk on spreads ≥ 9 points (NBA)
3. Verify external lines haven't moved significantly against your projection
4. Build entry in Entry Builder — check Pick'em Math panel
5. Review break-even vs your confidence level per leg

## Entry Construction Checklist
- ✅ Each leg P(Over) > break-even for the entry type
- ✅ No B2B or 3-in-4 players with high fatigue loads
- ✅ No extreme blowout risk (spread ≥ 18 NBA)
- ✅ No correlated same-team receivers (payout shift risk)
- ✅ EV indicator is green or amber, never red
- ✅ Logged to Journal before lock

## Post-Game
1. Update entry results in Journal (WIN/LOSS/PARTIAL)
2. Review Pick Hit Rate for the day
3. Flag any legs that hit/missed for calibration tracking
4. Note any surprises (injury scratch not caught, lineup change)
